Who We are

Chris' Carpet Cleaning Service is a fourth-generation, family-owned carpet cleaning service based in Plymouth Massachusetts. We strive to deliver the best carpet cleaning services possible to our clients. There is no stain too tough, no smell too smelly for our fully-licensed techs to remove.We use the latest in nuclear-powered carpet cleaners to fully eliminate any stains and smells in your carpet. Don't believe us? Make your carpet as filthy as possible and if we can't make it look as good as new, we will refund your money and buy you a new carpet!

Where We Service

We service the entirety of Plymouth County, as well as Cape Cod and the Islands.
